About Me

I have previously worked on a game called Voster Coaster, which will be a roller coaster building and tycoon game for VR headsets. This game is open source and available on GitHub at https://github.com/ajayyy/VosterCoasterVR

You can see many of my other projects I have worked on in the past on GitHub. I also participate in the tri-annual Ludum Dare game making competiton. I have participated in every competition since August 2014. You see these here as well as the older projects on their legacy site.

I am a former member of Lake Effect Robotics, a FIRST robotics team who won at the Detroit world championships in 2018. I created Android apps for keeping track, analysing, and distributing other teams' preformances. The app uses bluetooth to communitcate with a server device.

https://github.com/LakeEffectRobotics/LakeEffectScoutingApp
